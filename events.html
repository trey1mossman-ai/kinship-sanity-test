Weddings

Small guest list, big heart.

Celebrate your intimate wedding reception at Kinship Landing, a boutique hotel wedding venue in downtown Colorado Springs where evenings feel relaxed, personal, and entirely your own. Gather your favorite people in our light-filled Greenhaus, an indoor space designed for dining, toasts, and dancing for up to 80 guests, where candles glow and conversations linger.

Start with a rehearsal dinner or welcome party under the plants, move into cocktails in The Yard, then settle into a joyful, dance-it-out wedding reception that feels more like a great dinner party than a formal production. With downtown Colorado Springs right outside and Pikes Peak just beyond, Kinship is the kind of place where a wedding reception turns into a full, unforgettable weekend.

Meetings & Retreats

Less Boardroom, More Breakthrough

We bring teams together for offsites, planning sessions, and small group retreats designed to feel focused, comfortable, and connected.

This isn’t a fluorescent-lit ballroom. It’s a space where strategy sharpens, relationships strengthen, and people actually like being in the room. Our light-filled meeting and retreat spaces are built for conversation, collaboration, and momentum.

End the day around a table, not an inbox, with group room blocks, on-site dining, and space to stay together all in one place.

Room Blocks

Keep everyone close.

Kinship Landing offers room blocks in downtown Colorado Springs for weddings, corporate retreats, and group events, making it easy for guests to stay together and stay present. With thoughtfully designed rooms just steps from our meeting and event spaces, the experience flows naturally from conversations and celebrations to rest and reconnection.

Room blocks simplify planning and turn a gathering into a shared stay. No shuttles, no scattered hotels, just one place to meet, unwind, and wake up together. For larger groups or multi-day experiences, a full-hotel buyout creates a private, immersive setting where every moment belongs to your group.

Whether you’re hosting an intimate wedding weekend, leadership retreat, offsite, or family celebration, we’ll help you build a stay that feels effortless, connected, and unmistakably Kinship.
